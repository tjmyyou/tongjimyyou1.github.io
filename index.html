Biography

I am currently a Professor at College of Electronic and Information Engineering, Tongji University (Jiading Campus). I am Member of Machine Learning Committee of China Artificial Intelligence Society, member of Youth Working Committee of China Computer Society, professional member of China Computer Society. And I have long been engaged in research on Visual Perception , Robotic Learning and Image & 3D Content Generation, including Few-shot Object Detection and Recognition. I am also engaged in a number of projects such as National Natural Science Foundation, Shanghai Natural Science Foundation, Shanghai Science and Technology Innovation Action Plan and enterprise science and technology cooperation. My publications cover more than 50 papers in CVPR, AAAI, ICRA, TIP, PR, RAL, TITS, TIM and other well-known global conferences and journals.

I got my Bacheler, Master and Ph.D. degree from the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University in 2007, and my advisor is Prof. Chun Chen.

My research interests include Robotic Learning and Generative AI. Specifically, I work on

  • Visual Perception

  • Robotic Action Learning

  • Image & 3D Content Generation

  • Few-shot Object Detection and Recognition
